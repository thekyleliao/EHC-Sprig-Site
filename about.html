About Us!

Edison Hack Club consists of 70+ members at began operations at the start of the 2022-23 school year. Founders Kyle Liao, Sahibjot Singh, and Jayson Vasquez set out to create a meaningful student run club that brought CS exposure to Thomas A. Edison CTE HS. From September to now, we have had multiple workshops enabling members to learn CS and SE skills and collaborate with others, started work on collabrative club projects, and hope to make this our first successful event.
